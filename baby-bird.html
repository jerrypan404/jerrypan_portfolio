My Role

  • Produced an award-winning thesis short film from pre-production through festival strategy
  • Managed a seven-day production schedule across diverse filming locations in Boston

  • Created a child-friendly set environment for six actors, including two minors
  • Pitched and collaborated with Filmmakers Collaborative, a 501(c)(3) organization, for post-production fundraising
  • Supported the film's festival run, with selections including BUFF, Salem Horror, Final Girls Berlin, and more
